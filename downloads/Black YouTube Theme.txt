How to use a black theme for YouTube:

Install this CHROME extension:
https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/themes-for-youtube-and-pi/deljjimclpnhngmikaiiodgggdniaooh

Once installed, Right click on it and click "Options"
Scroll down to "Custom YouTube Theme" highlited in yellow
Paste everything from this link into the text box - https://pastebin.com/raw/5eQZ4Nkm

Hit "Save and Apply", and YouTube should refresh.
